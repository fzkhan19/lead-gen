# Design Context

## Users
Growth hackers, solo entrepreneurs, and elite sales teams looking for an autonomous acquisition engine.

## Brand Personality
Stealthy, precise, high-performance, and exclusive. It should feel like a "Ghost Protocol" mission control.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Deep blacks and dark zinc/slate neutrals.
- **Accents**: Sparing use of electric blue (`#3b82f6`) and white for high-contrast actions.
- **Typography**: Space Grotesk for bold, technical headings; Inter for clean UI text; JetBrains Mono for data and status codes.
- **Visual Style**: Glassmorphism with high blur, thin borders (1px), and subtle gradients.

## Design Principles
1. **Hierarchy through Lightness**: Use varying levels of gray/zinc to create depth, not just borders.
2. **Monospace Accents**: Use JetBrains Mono for data, status codes, and technical labels.
3. **Consistent Interaction**: All buttons must follow a strict primary/secondary/ghost hierarchy with consistent radii and hover states.
4. **Stealth Motion**: Transitions should be fast and smooth (ease-out-expo).
5. **Information Density**: High density but with clear breathing room between logical groups.
